AT HOME IN LIMASSOL

St. Andreas Hotel was designed to offer hospitality, comfort, affordable luxury and friendliness to its guests, within a cosmopolitan atmosphere. These are the key details of a boutique hotel, where visitors will get to meet the small staff team managing the hotel and will be welcomed with a warm smile.

One of the most unique elements of the hotel will be the "secret garden" which will spread across the restaurant and bar area. This space is specially designed as an all-day concept, which will host not only hotel guests but also visitors and passers-by who wish to enjoy their meal or drink in a historic city center.

The elements with the olive tree and other plants and flowers used both in the logo and the general identity of the hotel, are an extension of the secret garden and reflect its the Cypriot identity.

As the project under development, more details will be revealed in the future about its structure, internally and externally. All the amenities it has to offer to its visitors will be showcased as well.
